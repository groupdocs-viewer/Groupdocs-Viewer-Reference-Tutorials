## Getting Started Tips

**Start Simple**: Begin with common formats like SVG or APNG before tackling specialized formats like AI or CDR images. This helps you understand the basic workflow without complexity.

**Test Output Quality**: Always verify rendered output quality matches your requirements. Different formats may need specific rendering settings for optimal results.

**Consider Performance**: For high-volume applications, implement caching strategies and consider asynchronous processing to maintain responsive user experiences.

**Handle Errors Gracefully**: Implement proper error handling for unsupported or corrupted image files. Your application should fail gracefully and provide meaningful feedback to users.
